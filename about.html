About the Tour

A Celebration

Rolex Certified Pre-Owned Watches

The Coast to Coast Tour brings The 1916 Company's exceptional collection of Rolex Certified Pre-Owned watches directly to collectors and enthusiasts across the country.

Each event features a curated selection of more than 100 collectible Rolex timepieces, including beloved discontinued models, distinctive stone dials, gem-set cases, unique configurations, and more. It's a rare opportunity to experience these exceptional watches in person.
